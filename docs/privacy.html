Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

SignalDraft ("we," "us") respects your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website and services.

Information we collect

  • Research brief data: Company name, website, business objectives, audience details, and contact information you submit through our intake form.
  • Technical data: Standard web logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited) via our hosting provider.
  • Local storage: Draft intake data saved in your browser until you clear it or submit.

How we use information

  • To generate and deliver survey design services you request
  • To communicate about your project (status, revisions, delivery)
  • To improve our methodology and templates (anonymized, aggregated)

AI processing

We use AI (including xAI Grok) to research public business information and generate survey drafts. Brief data submitted to our systems is processed to produce your deliverables. We do not sell your data. Third-party AI providers process data per their terms; we minimize personal data sent to AI systems.

Data retention

Project data is retained for the duration of our business relationship plus 24 months, unless you request earlier deletion.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data. Contact hello@signaldraft.com.

GDPR / CCPA

For EU and California residents: we process data based on contract performance and legitimate interest. Survey instruments we design for your end respondents should include appropriate consent language — we include standard blocks by default.

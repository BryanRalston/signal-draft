About SignalDraft

Research methodology, accelerated by AI.

Most surveys fail before they're sent. Wrong questions. Leading phrasing. No connection between what the business needs to decide and what the instrument actually measures.

SignalDraft exists to close that gap. We're not a form builder — we're a research design service that uses AI to do the expensive part faster: understanding your business context, scanning competitors, and mapping market dynamics to a rigorous survey instrument.

How we're different

Typeform and SurveyMonkey generate questions from a prompt. Research agencies do the full job for $5,000 or more. We sit in the middle: context-aware instrument design with cited research, methodology annotations on every question, and human review before anything reaches your respondents.

Our methodology standards

  • Every question maps to a stated research objective
  • Validated scales where applicable (NPS, Gallup Q12, semantic differential)
  • Bias controls: polarity alternation, no double-barreled questions
  • Length optimization — we fight to shorten, not expand
  • GDPR/CCPA consent blocks built in from day one
  • Anonymity rules for employee surveys (5+ aggregation threshold)

AI-assisted, human-reviewed

AI handles research synthesis and draft generation. A human reviews every deliverable for methodology compliance before it ships. We tell you this upfront because trust matters more than magic.
